March 29th, 2003
09:06 pm: birfday bash
so yeah, thursday was prolly the most wild nite I'd had in a very long time. starts off with a nice little dinner with my friend robyn at tortillas. we started gettin rowdy in the restaurant. even made the crusty hardcore kid shake when he was giving us back our change. haha. then met up with the birthday boy, brad, at his place. rode to the local from his place on bike and met up with robyn there. had some beer/jager. started the fun. the ex-guitarist from malevolent creation (hah, I love throwing that in there cause its true!) stopped by to buy the birthday boy a drink. finished off our drinks and then biked on down to the brewhouse where we met up with the rest of the gang. drank some more, had some mammoth 2 pint glasses of guinness and more jager. wrastled an asian chick on the patio. then, we came up with the idea to get kicked out of 80s nite at the masquerade. well, one of us managed to get escorted out after spraying beer everywhere, pushing people, clotheslined 2 guys, and brad and I started a pit on the floor. haha. there are a lot of gaps in my memory due to the influence of alcohol but sometime later in the evening we ended up at the clermont lounge-- as usual.
