October 7th, 2002
08:55 am: last nite
hah. last nite was a blast. hung out with a buddy of mine. rode our bikes to a free show. mastodon played and some other band...duel time? not sure. excellent stoner sludge. while there, I couldnt resist the offer of a "bottomless cup of pbr." so I took advantage of that. a few hours passed. the show ended, so my friend and I decided to ride over to the majestic for some quick food. man, that was rough. 8 or 9 cups of pbr and a mile bike ride will really wind you. hahaha. yeah, so after that we rode back to his apt. on that ride, it really started to rain; pouring-ass rain. we got back to his place. that was probably the best bike ride though. ugh, man are my legs sore though. definitely need to do that again...minus the beer. ;)
