Today was one of those annoying days where you work hard on something. Spend hours. And then someone says one thing that nearly undoes it all. Maybe a little more dramatic sounding than it was, but it’s the principle of it all. In this case, I was working on a project but someone failed to mention one key thing that would have simplified everything. So I had to shift gears, putting me a little behind on things. Oh well.

Anyways, the days go by so fast. After work, we ate and then relaxed a minute. I tried to start a solo RPG called Dark Dungeons Delvers. Worked a little bit on creating the dungeon map before I had to quit. Vincent was doing homework and going a bit crazy with Cia, so I had to try to intervene before Erika lost her mind. Shortly after that, it was time to put the kids to bed. Cia really wanted to put on pjs, slippers, and hoodie. She “read” a few books to me. And then eventually fell asleep. It was pretty cute.
