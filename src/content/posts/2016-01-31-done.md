Starting to get the hang of Jekyll sites. At first, I wasn't exactly sure how everything fit together, what with partials and default pages and whatnot; but it's starting to all click now that I've been editing and fooling around with things. 

For isntance, earlier, I noticed that when I navigated to one of my other pages, my fonts weren't loading. I'm sure there's a million ways to get that to work, but I ended up just placing an **inlcude** on each page to pull in my head partials, and that seems to do the trick. 

While I was figuring all of this out, Lorn popped into my head and I decided to check out and see if he had any new material out. It's been about two or three years since I've listened to anything. The latest out album, "Vessel", came out sometime last year. It's really good, and the production is as polished and nice as ever. I'm not sure how to classify the music, but I can say that it draws influence from dark IDM, glitch, synth wave, and dare I say it, dub step.
