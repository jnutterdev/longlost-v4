After battling with github to host the blog, I decided to transfer it over to my hosting. Not sure why I didn't want to do that right away, but I can see it's the right choice. Everything just runs so much smoother and I don't have to deal with github's little quirks. Plus, bonus points for having a direct URL, rather than something with github.io at the end of it.
